Atomic Habits: An Easy & Proven Way to Build Good Habits & Break Bad Ones

  • Description: Atomic Habits is James Clear's most well-known work, focused on the idea of making small, incremental changes to improve habits over time. The book explains how habits are formed, how they can be changed, and provides a four-step model (Cue, Craving, Response, Reward) to help readers build better habits and break bad ones. The book has become a bestseller, praised for its actionable advice and science-based approach to behavior change.

  • Publication Date: October 16, 2018.
