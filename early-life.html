Early Life

James Clear, the author of Atomic Habits, was born in 1982 and grew up in Hamilton, Ohio. His early life was marked by a strong interest in sports, particularly baseball, in which he excelled and earned All-American honors twice during college.

Clear attended Denison University, where he graduated in 2008 with a degree in biomechanics. His passion for understanding human performance and habits took root during his time as an athlete, eventually guiding him toward writing about self-improvement and behavior change.

After graduating, Clear worked as a baseball coach and strength coach, where he deepened his interest in habits and performance improvement. He officially began his writing career in 2012, launching his website JamesClear.com, which became the platform for his insights on building good habits.

His work later culminated in the bestselling book Atomic Habits.
